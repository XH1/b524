SoCoG

      The social computing working group (SoCoG) is a research office under the teaching and research department of computing intelligence, college of computer science and technology, Jilin university, presided over and led by associate professor Xu Hao.It mainly studies human-computer interaction, knowledge engineering and data science in computer education, knowledge management and medical education.
